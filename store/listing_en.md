## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Football Dice brings the tension of a tabletop American football board game to your phone. No twitchy taps, no reflexes required — just play-calling, dice, and a matchup chart deciding every down.

🏈 HOW IT WORKS
• Pick an offense play from your playbook — Long Pass, Short Pass, Runningback Draw, and more
• Your opponent picks a defense in secret
• Roll the dice, resolve the matchup on the chart, and watch the yardage play out on the field
• Manage downs, distance, the clock, and 4th-down decisions (punt, field goal, or go for it) just like a real drive

🧠 PLAY SMART, NOT FAST
Football Dice is a game of information and probability, not reaction time. Every offense card has a real average-yardage profile, every defense card counters certain play types, and the dice chart rewards reading your opponent instead of clicking the fastest button.

🤖 THREE AI DIFFICULTIES
Practice against Easy, Normal, or Hard AI opponents. Higher difficulties call sharper defenses and adjust their play-calling based on what you've shown them.

📶 PLAY WITH A FRIEND
Host or join a match with a friend over the same Wi-Fi network — no account, no server, no internet plan required. Just two phones and a full 4-quarter game.

📖 FULL PLAYBOOK, YOUR CALL
Don't like the 3 suggested plays for your situation? Open the full offense or defense playbook and pick any card you want, any down.

⚙️ MADE THE WAY YOU WANT IT
• Korean and English support, switchable anytime
• Dice-roll and card-matchup animations you can turn on or off
• Sound effects you can mute independently
• Full box-score scoreboard, quarter tracker, and down-and-distance display

Whether you grew up flipping through a paper playbook and rolling dice on the kitchen table, or you're discovering tabletop football strategy for the first time, Football Dice keeps the strategy and cuts the setup.
